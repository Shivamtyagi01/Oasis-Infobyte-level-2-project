A Glimpse into the Legend's Career

With a basic understanding of his career, you can easily appreciate the genius of Virat Kohli. He is not just a cricketer; he is an emotion for millions. His dedication, aggressive style, and unmatched consistency have set new benchmarks in the world of cricket.

  • 2008 - Captained the India Under-19 team to victory at the 2008 U-19 World Cup.
  • 2008 - Made his One Day International (ODI) debut for India against Sri Lanka.
  • 2010 - Scored his first ODI century, hitting 107 against Sri Lanka.
  • 2011 - Was a part of the Indian squad that won the ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • 2012 - Appointed as the vice-captain of the ODI team and scored his career-best 183 against Pakistan in the Asia Cup.
  • 2013 - Reached the number one spot in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen for the first time.
  • 2014 - Took over the Test captaincy from MS Dhoni.
  • 2017 - Became the captain across all three formats of the game.
  • 2018 - Won the prestigious Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year.
  • 2023 - Broke Sachin Tendulkar's long-standing record by scoring his 50th ODI century, the most by any player in history.
